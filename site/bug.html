🐛 Bug Report

Crash, stack trace, or something visibly broken. If it's a balance complaint or awkward UI, use Issue instead.

How to grab the console log

Open an Explorer window and paste this into the address bar: 

%APPDATA%\Godot\app_userdata\Slay the Spire 2\logs

Open the most recent .log file, copy the contents (Ctrl+A → Ctrl+C), and paste into the Console Log field below. The last ~200 lines around the crash are usually enough — paste the whole thing if you aren't sure.
